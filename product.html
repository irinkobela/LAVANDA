Discover the refined charm of LAVANDA Jewelry — where minimalist elegance meets soulful design. Each piece is handcrafted from luminous 925 Sterling Silver, celebrating clean lines, organic forms, and meaningful details. Whether it’s a delicate chain, a soft dome ring, or a sculptural wrap, every item is made to feel both personal and timeless.

Designed to move with you, LAVANDA pieces are perfect for everyday wear or adding a quiet statement to special moments. Whether worn alone or layered, they’re a reflection of your unique story — a touch of silver that speaks without saying a word.

Materials:
All LAVANDA jewelry is made from high-quality 925 Sterling Silver — prized for its lasting shine and durability. Every piece is nickel-free and hypoallergenic, crafted with care and attention to detail.

Care Instructions:
To preserve your jewelry’s beauty:

  • Avoid contact with moisture, perfumes, lotions, and cleaning products.
  • Store it in a dry place — ideally in the original pouch or box.
  • Clean gently with a soft polishing cloth to maintain its natural brilliance.
